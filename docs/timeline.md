Timeline
========

**Spoiler warning!** This contains MAJOR SPOILERS for the campaign.


Prehistory
----------

* The demoness **Jézabel** enters Irdya.
* **An-Usrukhar**, the First and Greatest Mage, destroys and seals Jézabel.


465 YW
------

* **Iliah-Malal** unseals Jézabel during his quest. Jézabel opposes him and flees.
* Jézabel begins to restore her power by parasitizing others as a ghost.


849-852 YW
----------

* Lord **Roldur** of Lintanir colludes with a slaver for his wealth.
* **Tamar**, the Lady of Greenwood and Roldur's sister, discovers her brother's misdeeds. She confronts Roldur and kills herself in despair.
* Roldur schemes to prevent the truth of Tamar's death from being revealed.
* Lord **Mel Owain** begins to descend into madness following the loss of Tamar, his wife.


853 YW
------

* Mel Owain leaves Greenwood.
* **Arwen**, the remaining prince, leaves Greenwood and begins his wanderings.


854 YW
------

* Jézabel possesses Mel Owain and turns him to evil.


869 YW
------

* The undead led by Mel Owain launch a surprise attack on the Silent Forest. The remaining elves are almost completely destroyed.
* The Silent Forest becomes an undead stronghold concealed by magic.
* **Simone** rises and becomes a follower of Mel Owain.


870 YW
------

* Greenwood takes the first attack from the undead. **Ellrea** is captured by bandits after fleeing.
* Arwen and his band rescue Ellrea. **Greenwood's Tears** begins.
* The undead march through the Far North, laying waste to many places.
* Arwen returns to Greenwood, and a council is held.
* Arwen and the elvish hero **Kalenz** infiltrate the Silent Forest. The undead led by Simone are defeated, and their stronghold is destroyed.
* Arwen confronts Jézabel in the underground. Jézabel fails to open a portal to a different world and is destroyed.
* Mel Owain is destroyed, and the turmoil settles.
* Arwen assassinates Roldur and leaves Greenwood once again. **Greenwood's Tears** ends.
